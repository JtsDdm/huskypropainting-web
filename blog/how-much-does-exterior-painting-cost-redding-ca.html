How Much Does Exterior Painting Cost in Redding CA? (2025)

By Husky Pro Painting · Redding, CA · Updated June 2025 · 6 min read

If you're a homeowner in Redding or Shasta County thinking about repainting your home's exterior, the first question is always: how much is this going to cost? Having painted hundreds of homes in Northern California, we're giving you real numbers — not vague ranges from a national website that's never seen a Redding summer.

Quick answer: Most single-family homes in Redding, CA cost between $2,500 and $6,500 for a complete exterior paint job in 2025. The average 1,500–2,000 sq ft home runs around $3,500–$4,500 fully done.

What Affects the Price

1. Square footage of paintable surface

This is the biggest factor. We measure every paintable surface — siding, trim, fascia, soffits, doors, and shutters. A 2,000 sq ft floor plan doesn't mean 2,000 sq ft of paintable exterior. It can easily be 3,000+ sq ft when you include all the surfaces.

2. Current paint condition

A house with peeling, chalking or blistering paint requires significantly more prep work — scraping, sanding, and priming. This can add $500–$1,500 to the total cost, but skipping it means your new paint will fail within 2–3 years.

3. Surface material

Wood siding takes more prep than smooth stucco. Lap siding takes longer to paint than flat stucco. Brick requires special primers. Redding has a mix of all three, so your material matters.

4. Number of stories

Two-story homes require scaffolding or tall ladders, which adds time and labor cost. Expect a 20–35% premium over a comparable single-story home.

5. Number of colors

One body color + one trim color is standard. Every additional color adds masking and cutting time. Three or more accent colors can add $300–$800 to the job.

Why Redding Prices Are Different From National Averages

National cost guides often quote $1.50–$4.00 per square foot for exterior painting. In Redding's market, that range holds, but a few local factors matter:

  • Extreme heat: We can only paint during certain hours in summer to avoid paint drying too fast. This affects scheduling and sometimes labor hours.
  • UV exposure: Shasta County gets intense sun. We always recommend 100% acrylic exterior paint with high UV resistance — cheaper paints fade and chalk within 2–3 years here.
  • Wind and dust: Redding's wind means we need to be careful about dust contaminating fresh paint, which adds prep time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it worth repainting my house before selling in Redding?
Almost always yes. Fresh exterior paint typically returns $2–$4 for every $1 spent in resale value, especially in Redding's competitive real estate market. It's one of the highest-ROI home improvements you can make before listing.
How long does an exterior paint job last in Northern California?
With proper prep and premium 100% acrylic paint, a quality exterior paint job in Northern California should last 7–10 years. In harsh conditions (direct sun, wood siding), expect 5–8 years before you notice noticeable fading.
