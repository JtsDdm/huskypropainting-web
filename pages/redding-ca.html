Why Redding Homes Need
Specialized Painters

Redding consistently ranks as one of the hottest cities in the continental United States, with summer temperatures regularly exceeding 105°F. That level of heat is brutal on exterior paint — it accelerates fading, causes chalking, and leads to premature peeling when the wrong products or techniques are used.

UV radiation at this latitude is intense year-round. Paint that looks fine in San Francisco or Sacramento can blister within two summers in Shasta County. We've seen it happen on countless homes — and we've repainted many of them.

Husky Pro Painting works exclusively in Northern California. We've learned through experience which paint formulas, application techniques, and primer systems hold up under extreme conditions — and we apply that knowledge to every project we take on in Redding.

The result: paint jobs that last 7–10 years instead of the industry average of 4–6, and a warranty to back it up.
