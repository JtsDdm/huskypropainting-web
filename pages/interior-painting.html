Healthy Home

Zero-VOC Paints
Available at No Extra Cost

Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) are the chemicals responsible for that sharp paint smell — and for indoor air quality issues that can linger for days after painting. Traditional paints release VOCs as they dry, which can cause headaches and irritation, especially in poorly ventilated spaces.

We offer zero-VOC interior paints from Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore on all residential projects. These are especially recommended for bedrooms, nurseries, children's rooms, and homes with allergy or asthma sensitivities.

Zero-VOC does not mean lower quality — these are the same premium formulas used in hospitals and schools where air quality requirements are strict. You can re-occupy rooms within hours of painting.

How It Works

Our Interior Painting
Process

1

Free Estimate & Color Consult

We walk through every room, assess wall conditions, discuss your color preferences, and deliver a written line-item estimate within 24 hours. We bring paint samples and help you decide on finishes and sheens.

2

Furniture Protection & Masking

We move and cover furniture, lay canvas drop cloths on all floors, tape all trim and edges with precision masking tape. Your home is protected before a single brush stroke.

3

Surface Prep & Repairs

Patching nail holes, skim coating damaged areas, sanding rough surfaces, and priming where needed. Imperfections that aren't fixed show through paint — we fix them first.

4

Paint Application

Two finish coats on walls and ceilings. Trim cut-in by brush with sharp, clean lines. We work room by room and allow proper dry time between coats. No rushing, no shortcuts.

5

Cleanup & Final Walkthrough

We remove all masking, replace furniture, collect all materials, and vacuum. You inspect the finished work with us — any touch-ups are done before we leave. We leave your home cleaner than we found it.

Common Questions

Interior Painting
FAQs

How long does interior paint last? +

5 to 10 years depending on the room and traffic level. High-traffic areas like hallways and kitchens need repainting more often. Using premium washable paints — which we always use — extends the life significantly and makes maintenance cleaning easier.

How much does interior painting cost in Redding? +

Cost depends on the number of rooms, ceiling height, current surface condition, and whether cabinets or trim are included. We price every job individually with a free in-person estimate. Call (530) 777-6573 — we deliver a written quote within 24 hours of visiting your home.

Do I need to empty the room before you paint? +

No — we handle furniture moving as part of the job. We move items to the center of the room, cover everything with heavy drop cloths, and return your furniture to its original position when we're done. Clearing out personal items like lamps and art is appreciated but not required.

Can you paint while we're living in the home? +

Yes. Most interior paint projects are completed with homeowners living in the property. We work room by room, keep disruption minimal, and use zero-VOC products when the home will be occupied. With zero-VOC paints, rooms are safe to re-enter within a few hours of painting.

Do you paint ceilings? +

Yes. Ceilings are included in our full interior painting service. We apply flat white ceiling paint to hide imperfections, or can match a custom color if requested. Popcorn texture ceilings can be painted over or removed (discussed during the estimate visit).
