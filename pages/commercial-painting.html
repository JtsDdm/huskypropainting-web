Common Questions

Commercial Painting
FAQs

Can you paint our business without closing? +

Yes. We regularly work around active business operations. For occupied offices and retail, we work in phases — completing sections while staff and customers use other areas. We also offer after-hours and weekend crews. For exterior work, we typically work during business hours with appropriate safety perimeters that don't block customer access.

Do you provide certificates of insurance? +

Yes. We provide certificates of insurance (COI) before any commercial project begins. We carry commercial general liability insurance and workers' compensation. We can add property managers, landlords, or HOA boards as additional insureds upon request — a standard requirement for commercial contracting in California.

Do you handle HOA painting projects? +

Yes. We work with homeowners associations in Shasta County on common area maintenance, building exterior repaints, fence and carport painting, and multi-phase projects that cover multiple buildings over time. We maintain color consistency across phases and coordinate directly with the HOA board or property management company.

How do I get a commercial painting estimate? +

Call us at (530) 777-6573 or fill out our estimate form. We'll schedule a site walk at your convenience and provide a written proposal within 48 hours that covers scope, products, schedule, and pricing. No obligation to proceed.
