Why Palo Cedro Homes Need
Specialized Painters

Palo Cedro sits in the eastern Shasta County valley, where summer temperatures consistently reach 100–106°F and afternoon winds sweep across the Millville Plains. That combination of intense heat and wind accelerates paint wear — particularly on west and south-facing elevations that take the full force of the afternoon sun.

Many properties in Palo Cedro are custom-built homes on larger lots, often with mixed exterior materials — wood siding, stucco, board-and-batten, and masonry in the same structure. Each material requires a different primer and paint system, and cutting corners on any one of them leads to early failure.

Horse properties and agricultural lots often have outbuildings, fencing, and sheds that need painting alongside the main structure. We handle the full property — not just the house — so everything maintains a consistent, well-maintained appearance.

Palo Cedro homeowners tend to have high expectations. So do we. We use premium paint systems, detailed prep, and thorough quality checks — and back every project with a 5-year workmanship warranty.
