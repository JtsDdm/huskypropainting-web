What's Included in Every
Exterior Paint Job

Skipping steps is how paint fails early. We never skip. Every exterior project includes thorough preparation before we open a single can of paint.

🚿

Power Washing

We pressure-wash the entire exterior to remove dirt, mildew, chalking, and loose paint. Paint applied to a dirty surface won't last — this step is non-negotiable.

🔧

Surface Prep & Repair

Scraping, sanding, filling cracks, and repairing any wood rot or damaged areas. We caulk all gaps around windows, doors, and trim to prevent moisture intrusion.

🎨

Priming

All bare wood, repaired surfaces, and high-porosity areas receive the correct primer. Primer creates the bond between surface and topcoat — skipping it cuts paint life in half.

🏡

Siding & Body

Two coats of 100% acrylic exterior paint on all siding surfaces. We use formulas specifically rated for high-UV and high-heat exposure — designed to outlast Redding's climate.

✏️

Trim, Fascia & Eaves

All trim, fascia boards, eaves, and architectural details are painted with precision. Sharp lines and clean edges are what separate a professional paint job from a DIY attempt.

Doors & Accents

Front doors, garage doors, shutters, and accent features. These high-visibility elements receive extra attention — they're often the first thing visitors notice about your home.

Climate Context

Why Exterior Paint
Fails Faster in Redding

Redding is one of the hottest cities in the United States, with summer temperatures consistently above 105°F and over 300 sunny days per year. This extreme heat and UV exposure breaks down paint chemistry faster than in milder California climates.

Cheap paint applied without proper prep can look fine in the first year, then chalk, peel, and blister in year two or three. We've repainted many homes in Redding that were painted by contractors using the wrong products for this climate.

We use only 100% acrylic formulas with UV stabilizers and heat-resistant binders — the same technology used on commercial buildings in desert climates. Combined with thorough surface prep, these products routinely last 7 to 10 years in Shasta County conditions.

The 5-year warranty we offer is not a marketing line — it is our commitment backed by the quality of materials and process we use on every job.

How long does exterior paint last in Redding? +

When done right, 7 to 10 years. Redding's extreme heat and UV exposure are the main enemies of exterior paint. Call (530) 777-6573 and we'll schedule a visit and deliver a written estimate within 24 hours.

What paint brands do you use? +

We use premium 100% acrylic exterior paints from Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore — brands with proven track records in high-heat, high-UV climates. We do not use budget-tier materials on any project. The paint brand and product line is disclosed in your estimate before work begins.
