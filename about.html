Who We Are

A Redding Painter
You Can Trust

Husky Pro Painting was founded by Chris, a Northern California native who has spent years mastering the craft of exterior and interior painting in one of the most demanding climates in the country. Redding's extreme heat, intense UV, and dry conditions make painting here harder than almost anywhere else — and that challenge is exactly what drove Chris to develop the techniques and product knowledge that define our work today.

We are a small, owner-operated company. Chris oversees every project personally — from the initial estimate to the final walkthrough. That means no subcontractors, no crew you've never met showing up at your door, and no shortcuts taken when no one is looking.

Our service area is Redding and Shasta County. We don't try to cover 50 cities. That focused approach means we show up on time, finish on schedule, and stand behind our work — with a 5-year workmanship warranty on every project we complete.

We also believe in transparency. Every estimate is written, line-item, and covers exactly what will be done and what it will cost. No surprises on move-in day. No vague "we'll see how it goes" pricing.
