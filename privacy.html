Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026 · Husky Pro Painting · Redding, CA

Husky Pro Painting ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website huskypropainting.com. This page explains what information we collect when you visit our site or contact us, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We take privacy seriously and will never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact information — name, phone number, email address, and property address when you request an estimate or contact us through our website forms.
  • Project information — details about your painting project, property type, and service needs that you share with us.
  • Communications — messages you send us via the website chat widget, contact form, email, or text.

We also collect certain information automatically when you visit our website:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, referring URLs, and browser/device type. This data is anonymous and aggregated.
  • Cookies — small data files stored in your browser used to improve site functionality and analyze traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to estimate requests and schedule in-person visits to your property.
  • Contact you with project updates, invoices, and follow-up communications.
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors find and use our services.
  • Comply with legal obligations as a licensed California contractor.

We do not use your personal information for unsolicited marketing beyond communicating about services you have requested or expressed interest in.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share it only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers — third-party tools we use to operate our business (e.g., GoHighLevel CRM, Google Analytics) that process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and property.
  • Business transfers — if Husky Pro Painting is sold or merges with another business, customer data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses third-party services that may collect data independently under their own privacy policies. These include Google Analytics (website traffic analysis), GoHighLevel (CRM and booking), Google Fonts (typography), and Google Maps (location services). We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services if you have concerns about their data practices.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. Our website uses HTTPS encryption. However, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of information you transmit to us.

6. Your Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information).
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at (530) 777-6573 or through our website contact form.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of those changes.
